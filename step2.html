Step #2 - Check Your Criminal Record

To get your criminal record sealed you will need to obtain a copy of your criminal record

Were you arrested in the District of Columbia?

According to D.C. Code § 2-1402.66, a person may request a copy of his or her arrest record to find out whether he or she is eligible to have that record “sealed".

Obtaining a copy of your criminal record online from DC Court
